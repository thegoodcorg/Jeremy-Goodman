As a tinkerer from birth, I've always enjoyed taking things apart and putting them back together again. After years of working as an automotive repairman, I discovered my passion for software development.

In my previous career, I honed my skills in problem-solving, troubleshooting, and diagnostics. I enjoyed the challenge of solving complex and interconnected issues and making the sum greater than that of its parts.

Now, as a software developer, I bring those same skills to the world of technology. I love the creative problem-solving and critical thinking that software development requires.

I am always eager to learn and expand my skillset, and I'm excited to see where my journey in software development takes me next.

When I'm not busy tinkering with code or working on my latest project, you can usually find me playing video games with friends. I'm a big fan of co-operative games that require teamwork and communication to succeed. There's nothing quite like the feeling of working together to achieve a shared goal!

Another one of my passions is working with dogs. I'm a proud owner of a corgi named Arthur, and I love spending time training him and teaching him new tricks. I have a deep compassion for animals and enjoy helping others with their furry friends whenever I can.

When I'm not at my computer or spending time with my dog, I'm usually on the ice playing hockey. I've been playing in one of the local leagues for years, and I love the thrill of the game and the camaraderie of my teammates. Hockey has taught me the importance of discipline, hard work, and perseverance, values that I carry with me in all aspects of my life.

In my personal life, I strive to be a reliable friend and someone that others can count on. Whether it's lending a listening ear or offering a helping hand, I believe in the importance of supporting those around me.
